  • Mars Attacks

    This is the first game I built using front-end technologies HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Each card responds to a click by flipping to reveal a symbol on the other side. Only two cards can be chosen at a time. If the cards match, they go up in flames, if they do not, they flip back to their original position.
